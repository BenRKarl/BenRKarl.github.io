    • Hi friend! Welcome to my website. My name is Ben Karl, and I just left my career to become a web developer. Crazy! I was in public relations for about seven years, working with companies such as Sony Playstation, PayPal, Toshiba, Intuit and, most recently, Jameson Irish Whiskey. Prior to working in PR I was an editorial intern at GamePro magazine, where I blogged for Games.net and ran a sister blog dedicated to Metal Gear Solid (nerdy, I know). When I was at GamePro I created a video that got pretty popular on the Internet for a few days. It's pretty embarrassing, but I still like to share it with people. If you don't know anything about Metal Gear Solid it will probably make zero sense to you since it's pretty wacky.

    Before working at GamePro I was a student at U.C. Berkeley, where I was a sprinter on the Track and Field team. At Cal I majored in Interdisciplinary Studies and wrote two research papers on interactive media (aka video games). The first was an analysis of the game Half-Life: Counter Strike in which I argued that social media elements (such as persistent identity and reputation) could be incorporated into online video games to decrease cheating. The second was my senior thesis in which I analyzed the ways certain classic video games could be broken down to create models for accelerated learning.
